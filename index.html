Why Your Website Domain Matters

Your website domain is your only permanent, self-owned digital asset. Everything else in your digital presence operates on rented platforms.

A domain gives you:

  • full ownership
  • long-term continuity
  • independence from platform changes
  • direct access to your audience

A short, profession-aligned domain also improves recall, clarity, and ease of access. The .doctor extension makes your profession immediately clear.

Self-Owned Website Domain
